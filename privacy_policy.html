2. What This App Provides

COSTEL & ELY Painting is a company information and service guidance app for painting and decorating services.

The app may provide:

  • Information about COSTEL & ELY PAINTING AND DECORATING LTD.
  • Painting and decorating service descriptions.
  • Service offers and guide prices.
  • Colour ideas and colour preview guidance.
  • A paint cost estimator for approximate area, paint quantity, material cost, labour cost and total guide cost.
  • Company contact information.
  • Privacy Policy and GDPR / UK & EU rights information.

3. Information We Collect

The COSTEL & ELY Painting app is designed as an informational and estimation tool. The app itself does not require users to create an account or log in.

Based on the current app design, the app does not include:

  • User account registration.
  • In-app login.
  • Advertising SDKs.
  • Analytics SDKs.
  • Firebase tracking.
  • WebView browsing.
  • Device location collection.
  • Camera, contacts, SMS, phone call, microphone or storage permissions.

The estimator may ask users to enter practical project values such as wall width, wall height, number of walls, doors, windows and coats. These values are used inside the app to calculate an estimate and are not intended to identify the user.

4. Contact Outside the App

If you choose to contact the company by phone, email or through the website, your communication will take place outside the app. In that case, the information you choose to provide, such as your name, phone number, email address, property details or project description, may be used only to respond to your enquiry, provide customer support, arrange service communication or prepare a quotation.

5. Estimate and Price Guidance

Any prices, service offers or estimates shown in the app are provided for guidance only. They are not a final quote and do not create a service agreement.

Final quotations may depend on factors including project size, measurements, wall condition, access, materials, preparation work, location, labour needs and on-site assessment.

Where you contact the company directly outside the app, your information may be used to:

  • Respond to your enquiry.
  • Discuss painting and decorating services.
  • Prepare or follow up on a quotation.
  • Provide customer service.
  • Keep reasonable business records where required.
  • Comply with applicable legal or regulatory obligations.

7. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information.
